    - ## Our Commitment to Child Safety
   
    - BAFLY is committed to protecting children from child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This document outlines our standards and policies regarding child safety within our application.
   
    - ## Definition of CSAE
   
    - Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) includes any form of sexual abuse, grooming, exploitation, or material depicting minors in sexual situations. BAFLY has zero tolerance for any such content.
   
    - ## Our Safety Standards
   
    - ### 1. Content Moderation
   
    - - All user-generated content is subject to review and moderation
     
      - - Content suspected of involving CSAE is immediately removed
       
        - - Accounts involved in CSAE are permanently banned
         
          - ### 2. User Protection
         
          - - Minors are protected through age-appropriate safety features
           
            - - Reporting mechanisms are available within the app
             
              - - Privacy settings protect user data
               
                - ### 3. Community Guidelines
               
                - - Users must comply with all applicable laws
                 
                  - - Prohibited content includes any CSAE material
                   
                    - - Violations result in account suspension or termination
